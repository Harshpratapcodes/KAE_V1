## 💡 Quick Actions

### View a Customer Lead
1. Go to `/admin/enquiries`
2. Click **"View"** on an enquiry
3. See full details
4. Send email or call directly

### Add a New Product
1. Go to `/admin/products`
2. Click **"Add Product"**
3. Fill in details
4. Click **"Save"**

### Write a Blog Article
1. Go to `/admin/blog`
2. Click **"Write New Article"**
3. Enter title and content
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Check Dashboard Stats
1. Go to `/admin`
2. See KPI cards at top
3. View 30-day trends
4. Check recent leads

---

## 🔑 Key Features at a Glance

### Dashboard (`/admin`)
- **KPIs**: New Leads, Total Leads, Conversion Rate, Active Products
- **Charts**: Lead trends, Leads by product
- **Recent Leads**: Latest customer enquiries

### Enquiries (`/admin/enquiries`)
- **Filter**: New, Contacted, Qualified
- **Quick Actions**: Email, Call, View Details
- **Status Tracking**: Update lead status
- **Notes**: Add internal notes

### Products (`/admin/products`)
- **Search & Filter**: Find products easily
- **CRUD Operations**: Create, read, update, delete
- **Product Info**: Name, category, price, rating

### Blog (`/admin/blog`)
- **Write**: Create articles from scratch
- **Publish**: Make articles live
- **Manage**: Edit or delete existing posts
- **Analytics**: View article views

## 💼 Common Workflows

### Handle a New Lead
```
1. Dashboard → See new lead notification
2. Go to Enquiries
3. Click View on the lead
4. Review customer details
5. Send email reply
6. Change status to "Contacted"
7. Add follow-up date
```

### Publish a Blog Article
```
1. Go to Blog Management
2. Click "Write New Article"
3. Fill Title & Content
4. Add SEO Meta Description
5. Review & Click "Save Article"
6. Article now published
```

### Launch a New Product
```
1. Go to Products
2. Click "Add Product"
3. Enter Name, Category, Price
4. Add Specifications
5. Upload Product Image
6. Click "Save"
7. Product appears on website
```
